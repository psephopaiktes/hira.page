### A full set of [Switchbot](https://amzn.to/3S74OF8)

I switched almost all the IoT devices in my room to Switchbot's. Specifically, things like:

* [Smart Lock](https://amzn.to/3tIAKX2)
* [Smart Hub](https://amzn.to/3TQYLG7)
* [Curtain](https://amzn.to/3vtSohO)
* [SwitchBot (button presser)](https://amzn.to/3TQYLG7)
* [Humidifier](https://amzn.to/3TNoGhT)
* [LED Bulb / LED Strip](https://amzn.to/41R9Xob)
* [Smart Plug](https://amzn.to/47n03eR)

Up until now I'd been buying cheap Chinese-made products one at a time, and each manufacturer had its own app to configure things — it was a real hassle. Now I can manage everything with just the Switchbot and Google Home apps, which is super easy and satisfying. The look is also nice and clean, and it can show the room's temperature and humidity.

![switchbot](./switchbot.png)

For example, when I say "good night" to Google Home, it runs a scene like the one below. If you have lots of home appliances like a circulator or accent lighting, I highly recommend it.

>Turn off all the lights, close the curtains, keep the bedroom humidity above 40% with the humidifier, schedule the AC to turn off in 7 hours



## Daily Life

### [NIUM CLASSIC](https://amzn.to/3vygeJ0)

![nium](./nium.jpg)

I tried various famous slim wallets, and this one became my best. The setup is almost the same as [the one I introduced before](/blog/201901_cashless), but it's incredibly small and unobtrusive — fantastic.

I also tucked an [Anker GPS card](https://amzn.to/3vygtno) inside, so I'm covered if I lose it.


### [THREE Eyebrow Pencil](https://www.threecosmetics.com/onlineshop/g/gt4b052/)

I have atopic dermatitis, so I can't really do full-on makeup. A stylish friend recommended that "just doing eyebrows is high impact for low effort", and I've been following that advice. * Note that you also need refills.

### Manga
* [Chikita★GUGU](https://video.unext.jp/book/title/BSD0000483296/BID0000818431)
    * It's a manga drawn by someone I know. I read it again after several years and found it incredibly fun, so I'll plug it here. It's a cute, surreal world with light gags, but it gets gory and heavy here and there.
* [Zeikin de Katta Hon (Books Bought with Tax Money)](https://video.unext.jp/book/title/BSD0000499999/BID0000851179)
    * I mentioned this last year too — it's nice that there are more manga lately that are both genuinely educational and fun.
* [Bengoshi no Kuzu (Lawyer Scumbag)](https://video.unext.jp/book/title/BSD0000036374/BID0000058634)
    * A famous one that's also been adapted into a drama, but I only just read it. It dives deep into legal knowledge while also having a great story.
