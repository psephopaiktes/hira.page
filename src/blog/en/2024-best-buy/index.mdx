## Gadgets

### [Rapoo mini mouse](https://amzn.to/3DNUTjc)

I wanted a mouse to leave permanently in my backpack, so I bought this. It has no notable features, just the bare minimum, but it looks good and is sufficient.

Among several small mice I've tried (I bought about three), this is just the least bad one. I'd be happiest if a new version of the [LG Ultra Thin Mouse](https://amzn.to/4agtFhl) came out though...

### [SwitchBot Air Purifier Table](https://px.a8.net/svt/ejp?a8mat=3ZJWF2+A9Q7G2+4W9U+BW0YB&a8ejpredirect=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.switchbot.jp%2Fproducts%2Fswitchbot-air-purifier-table)

<EmbedCard
    url="https://px.a8.net/svt/ejp?a8mat=3ZJWF2+A9Q7G2+4W9U+BW0YB&a8ejpredirect=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.switchbot.jp%2Fproducts%2Fswitchbot-air-purifier-table"

A SwitchBot air purifier. It's a great device that doubles as ambient lighting, a wireless charger, and a side table. Being able to automatically turn it on and off when I come home or leave is also convenient.

The great thing about this type is that you can naturally place it in the center of the room. The coverage area spec for an air purifier is actually based on **placing it in the center of the room**, so when you push it to the edge, you only get half the power.

If you don't already use SwitchBot for everything, the [LG AeroFurniture](https://amzn.to/42asY7a) series might be better in terms of design.

I'm starting off with a knockoff and that's quite something, sorry — but it was good.

I'd seen a stylish tension-rod rack called [DRAW A LINE](https://amzn.to/4gSE316) at a friend's place and thought it looked nice, but with my move I didn't have the money for the over-30,000-yen rack and was hesitating.

Follow-up products from [IRIS](https://amzn.to/40yxjjq) and [Yamazen](https://amzn.to/3C8mdbk) had also come out, but the rack from [Y.M.WORLD](https://a.r10.to/h5nDtm) looked good, so I ended up buying it.

I use it together with a [generic clamp light](https://amzn.to/4gQgTbL) I bought on Amazon (the bulb is automated with SwitchBot). The [light alone from the official brand](https://amzn.to/4gSE5Gg) might also be fine.

### [Microwave rice cooker thing](https://amzn.to/4ahdPCN)

<EmbedCard
    url="https://amzn.to/4ahdPCN"

It cooks rice properly and tastefully. If your travel hotel has a microwave, eating freshly cooked rice in the room is also fun.

## Content

### [I Want to Die Alone (manga)](https://amzn.to/40eBwap)

<EmbedCard
    url="https://amzn.to/40eBwap"
    img="https://m.media-amazon.com/images/I/91x3WtJdkVL._SY522_.jpg"
    title="Hitori de Shinitai (1) (Morning Comics) | Kaoru Kareizawa, Misaki Donelly | Seinen manga | Kindle Store | Amazon"
    site="www.amazon.co.jp" />

This is personally my best manga read of 2024.

The story starts when the protagonist, a single woman in her 30s working in an office, begins to reflect on her life after her aunt dies alone. Marriage, caregiving, inheritance, asset building, and so on — many topics covered carefully at the level of a how-to book.

Even though the themes sound heavy, **it's super comedic and entertaining**. At the same time, there are many unexpected developments that pull you in.

It looks like it's getting a drama adaptation this year.
