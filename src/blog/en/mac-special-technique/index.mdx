If you opened this article, you might be the type who **loves productivity tips** and gets excited learning about keyboard shortcuts or hidden features they didn't know about (and you're welcome even if you're not). I'm one of those people too, so I often chat with others about this kind of thing or dig through the official help docs.

The goal of this article is to round up <b>useful little tricks that even people of that type surprisingly don't know about, and that are genuinely handy</b>. "Hey, you should also know about this!" suggestions are welcome too, so feel free to let me know on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/psephopaiktes).

By the way, in this article I'll write keyboard shortcuts using the icons below, matching Apple's official notation. Example: pressing "command + shift + 4" together → `⌘⇧4`.

Icon | Key
:--:|:--:
⇧ | shift
^ | control
⌘ | command
⌥ | option
⌫ | delete
⇪ | caps lock
↩︎ | return
⇥ | tab
⎋ | esc
